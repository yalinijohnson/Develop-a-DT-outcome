At Elim Christian College, we believe that protecting the environment is a part of God's creation. Our Enviro group is dedicated to learning, sharing and practicing sustainable ways so that we can make our Earth a better place. Genesis 2:15 states that God did not put Adam and Eve just to dominate Earth but to nurture, protect and care for it. As students from a Christian school, we should protect and care for the environment just like Adam and Eve instead of polluting it.
